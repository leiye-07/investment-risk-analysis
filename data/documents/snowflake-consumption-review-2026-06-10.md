# Snowflake Consumption Review

Date: 2026-06-10
Entity: Snowflake

## Consumption Trends

Snowflake customer usage is improving after earlier optimization pressure, but revenue visibility remains sensitive to enterprise data workload growth. Large customers continue to scrutinize cloud spend, which can slow consumption expansion during budget reviews.

## Competitive Position

Competition from hyperscale cloud data platforms may pressure net revenue retention if customers consolidate workloads. Snowflake's AI features could support demand, but monetization is still early and may require sustained sales investment.

## Financial Risk

Operating margin improvement depends on disciplined hiring and infrastructure efficiency. A renewed slowdown in consumption would create downside risk to revenue growth and free cash flow expectations.
