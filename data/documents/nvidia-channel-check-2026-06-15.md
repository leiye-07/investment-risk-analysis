# NVIDIA Channel Check

Date: 2026-06-15
Entity: NVIDIA

## Demand Signals

Hyperscale demand for NVIDIA accelerator capacity remains strong, but some enterprise customers are delaying cluster expansion until power availability and rack-level cooling constraints improve. Integrators noted that lead times have normalized for some networking components while advanced packaging remains a source of supply risk.

## Margin And Competition

Gross margin risk is rising as customers compare NVIDIA systems with custom ASIC alternatives and lower-cost inference deployments. Price pressure is not broad-based yet, but procurement teams are asking for larger commitments before accepting premium pricing.

## Monitoring Items

Track customer concentration, advanced packaging capacity, export restrictions, and evidence that inference workloads are shifting toward lower-cost architectures.
