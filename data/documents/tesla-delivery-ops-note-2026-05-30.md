# Tesla Delivery And Operations Note

Date: 2026-05-30
Entity: Tesla

## Volume And Pricing

Tesla delivery growth may remain under pressure if price reductions are needed to defend market share in China and Europe. Lower average selling prices could weigh on automotive gross margin even if unit volume stabilizes.

## Execution Risks

Inventory days have increased in several regions, suggesting demand may be less predictable than production planning assumes. Launch timing for lower-cost models remains a key risk because delays could leave Tesla more exposed to competition in mass-market EV segments.

## Offsets

Energy storage demand and software attach rates may partially offset auto margin pressure, but the timing and durability of those cash flows remain uncertain.
